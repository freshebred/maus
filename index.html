Artist Statement

The art and code in this project is drawn and written digitally by Nam Hoang as an artistic project responding to Q3 MAUS research project.

This piece depicts Vladek in his prisoner’s suit, staring soullessly at the reader, illustrating his blankness of what he had gone through. His mental clock had forever stopped in 1944, during the war and can never escape the war inside his mind despite peacetime had come.

Open-source code at https://github.com/freshebred/maus.git
